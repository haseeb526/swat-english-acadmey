Purpose of PTM

The Parent-Teacher Meeting is an important platform where parents and teachers come together to discuss the student’s academic progress, behavior, and overall development. It fosters a collaborative relationship to help students achieve their best.

Schedule & Guidelines

  • Frequency: PTMs are held twice every academic year – mid-term and end-term.
  • Timing: 3:00 PM to 6:00 PM on scheduled dates announced via the school website and notice board.
  • Venue: School auditorium and respective class teachers’ rooms.
  • Attendance: All parents/guardians are requested to attend for constructive feedback.
  • Appointment: Please schedule your meeting slot in advance through the school office or online portal.
Please ensure punctuality to respect all appointments and maximize the effectiveness of the meetings.

Discussion Topics

  • Student academic performance and areas for improvement
  • Classroom behavior and social interaction
  • Attendance and punctuality
  • Suggestions for extracurricular activities and overall growth
  • Addressing any concerns from parents or teachers
